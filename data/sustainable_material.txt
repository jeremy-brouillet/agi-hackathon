Project 7: Apple Sustainable Materials Initiative
Description: Apple is committed to sustainability and is working on a project to develop cutting-edge materials for its products. This initiative aims to replace traditional plastics and metals with sustainable alternatives, reducing the environmental impact of Apple's products.
OKRs:
	•	Objective: Lead the industry in sustainable materials usage.
	•	Key Result 1: Develop a biodegradable smartphone casing material.
	•	Key Result 2: Eliminate the use of rare earth metals in Apple devices.
	•	Key Result 3: Ensure that 90% of Apple products use recyclable or renewable materials within five years.
