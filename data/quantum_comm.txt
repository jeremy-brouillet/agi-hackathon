Project 9: Apple Quantum Communications
Description: Apple's Quantum Communications project aims to secure data transmission using quantum key distribution (QKD). This technology will provide unprecedented levels of security, protecting user data from quantum computing-based attacks.
OKRs:
	•	Objective: Pioneer quantum-safe communication solutions.
	•	Key Result 1: Develop a commercially viable quantum encryption system.
	•	Key Result 2: Partner with major corporations to implement quantum-safe communication protocols.
	•	Key Result 3: Provide quantum-safe communication solutions to government agencies and critical infrastructure providers.
